Welcome to Vintage Nest Hotels

Vintage Nest stands out like a rose amidst tulips on Ilo-Awela road, Ota. It stands out not just in structure but in its growing reputation as the benchmark of excellent hospitality services in and around Ota. The homely fragrance of its ambience and the royal treatment it metes to its clients tells it all.

From its gate you are ushered into the warm reception of the porters who bear your luggage, then the receptionists whose priority is your care and comfort, and subsequently, the waiters that treat you to vintage services in the most courteous manner.
